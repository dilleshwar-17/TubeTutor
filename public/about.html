The 45 Day Journey

Mastering Data Structures & Algorithms, one day at a time.

The Mission

The 45 Day DSA Calendar is designed to help software engineers and students build a solid foundation in computer science fundamentals. We believe that consistency is the key to mastering complex topics like dynamic programming, graph theory, and system design.

How it Works

Our curriculum is divided into 5 distinct phases, each focusing on a specific layer of algorithmic thinking:

  • Phase 1: Foundation — Arrays, Strings, and basic sorting/searching.
  • Phase 2: Core DS — Linked Lists, Stacks, Queues, and Trees.
  • Phase 3: Algorithms — Advanced Graph theory and Dynamic Programming.
  • Phase 4: Advanced — Greedy, Backtracking, and Bit Manipulation.
  • Phase 5: Revision — Mock interviews and weak-spot refinement.

Features

We provide a suite of tools to keep you motivated and on track:

  • Interactive Tracking — Save your progress locally or sync it across devices.
  • Google Authentication — Seamless login to keep your data safe.
  • Visual Feedback — Progress bars, streaks, and confetti celebrations.
  • Glassmorphic UI — A premium, distraction-free environment for focused learning.
